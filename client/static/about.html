About

Tech Horizon Labs is run by Huxley Peckham, based in Noosa Heads on the Sunshine Coast. We build AI systems for small and mid-sized businesses across Queensland and Australia — the kind of teams that are growing fast and need their operations to keep up.

A construction company spent $45K on an AI vendor who delivered a chatbot nobody used. Great slides, working demos — but nobody checked whether the company's data was even in a format AI could read. That's the story I kept hearing. Expensive tools dropped into businesses that weren't ready for them. So I started THL to do the opposite: map the workflows first, find the real bottleneck, and build something that actually fits.

We've deployed systems for accounting firms, builders, real estate agencies, manufacturers, allied health clinics, talent agencies, and energy companies. We've trained teams of 2 and teams of 26. We pick the right tool — Claude, ChatGPT, open-source models, custom builds — based on what the job needs, not what we resell. If AI isn't the answer, we'll say so. We turn away about 30% of prospects because the timing isn't right or the ROI doesn't justify it.
