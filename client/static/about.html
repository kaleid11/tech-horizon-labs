Huxley Peckham, AI Systems Consultant

Tech Horizon Labs is run by Huxley Peckham, based in Noosa Heads on the Sunshine Coast. We build AI systems for small and mid-sized businesses across Queensland and Australia, the kind of teams that are growing fast and need their operations to keep up.

Huxley's background is in IT systems and blockchain engineering, work where infrastructure decisions are permanent and cutting corners costs more to unwind than to get right the first time. Before THL, he worked across software development, distributed systems, and enterprise technology. That engineering discipline shapes every THL engagement: start with the data, understand the workflows, then build automation that has something solid to run on.

We've deployed systems for accounting firms, builders, real estate agencies, manufacturers, allied health clinics, talent agencies, and energy companies. We've trained teams of 2 and teams of 26. We pick the right tool — Claude, ChatGPT, open-source models, custom builds — based on what the job needs, not what we resell.
