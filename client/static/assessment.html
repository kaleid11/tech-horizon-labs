      Frequently Asked Questions

      What does the AI readiness score mean?

      Your score out of 100 maps to one of four AI maturity stages: Discovery (0–25), ChatGPT Plateau (26–50), Systematically Enabled (51–75), and Fully AI-Native (76–100). Only 5% of Australian SMBs reach Stage 4. The score tells you where you are relative to other Australian businesses and what to prioritise next.

      Who is this assessment for?

      The assessment is designed for Australian small and medium business owners and managers with 5–50 employees. It covers data readiness, team AI literacy, process documentation, security posture, and technology adoption speed. It's relevant across all industries including construction, accounting, healthcare, legal, and manufacturing.

      How long does the assessment take?

      Approximately 3 minutes. 10 multiple-choice questions covering your AI strategy, data infrastructure, team readiness, budget, and technology adoption patterns. Results are instant and personalised.

      Is the assessment really free?

      Yes, completely free. You receive your score, maturity stage, and personalised next-step recommendations immediately. We ask for your email to send a copy of your results and our fortnightly AI newsletter, which you can unsubscribe from at any time.

      What happens after I complete the assessment?

      You get instant results showing your AI maturity stage and three prioritised recommendations. You can then download our free report to benchmark against 54 other Australian businesses, or book a free 15-minute discovery call to discuss your specific opportunities.

      How does this compare to the free discovery call?

      The self-assessment gives you a data-driven benchmark of where your business sits. The free 15-minute discovery call is a live conversation where we map your specific workflows and identify your highest-impact automation opportunity. The assessment gives you a score; the call gives you a plan.
