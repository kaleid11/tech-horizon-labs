Work

Every project here is something we built and deployed for a real business. No hypotheticals.

Creator Economy · Brisbane

Talent agency managing 270+ YouTube creators across AU, EU, and LATAM. 15 staff. The team was manually checking contracts, calculating rates across flat-rate and CPM deal structures, and drafting emails in inconsistent voices. Onboarding calls generated transcripts nobody had time to review.

Built a full AI infrastructure inside their Claude Teams workspace: an email drafter with individual voice profiles for each team member using the CTRA framework. An agreement checker that queries their entire contract library instead of manually opening Drive folders. A roster and rate engine — one question returns the full creator picture, pulling live from Google Sheets and HubSpot (12,000+ deals). A tiered knowledge base separating admin-only content (financials, margins, escalation rules) from team-level access. Meeting intelligence from onboarding call transcripts.

Delivered a 32-section technical analysis covering stakeholder matrices, deal economics, competitive benchmarking, and full architecture. All 15 staff trained in weekly workshops.

Energy · Vault Energy

Needed market intelligence on renewable energy costs per region. The research required manually pulling data from multiple government and international databases — ABS, energy market APIs, World Bank.

Built a market research agent using OpenClaw, plugged into ABS, RapidAPI energy endpoints, and World Bank data. Automated regional cost analysis that previously required days of manual research across scattered sources.
