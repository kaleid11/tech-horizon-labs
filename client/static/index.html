The Horizon Method

01
Map
1–2 weeks

We map how your business actually runs. Every tool, workflow, bottleneck, and opportunity — before we touch anything. You get The Horizon Report: an operational audit with prioritised AI opportunities and ROI estimates. Not a deck of generalities. Specifics.

02
Blueprint
2–4 weeks

We design the architecture: which tools, which workflows, how they connect. We build your Horizon Brain — the knowledge system your AI runs on, with personal, business, and client layers separated. You get the Architecture Blueprint.

03
Deploy
4–8 weeks

We build the systems, train your team, go live. Not a prototype. Not a recommendation. Working systems, integrated with your existing tools, deployed into your workflow. Every person gets AI workflows for their actual job. We don't leave until the team owns it.

04
Retain
Ongoing

We stay on as your AI team. Monitoring, optimising, shipping new systems as your business and the technology evolve. Weekly calls. 24-hour async SLA. A living AI roadmap that updates as the tools do.

Selected Work

Creator Economy · Brisbane

Talent agency managing 270+ YouTube creators across AU, EU, and LATAM. 15 staff. Team was manually checking contracts, calculating rates across different deal structures, and drafting emails in inconsistent voices.

Built a suite of Claude-powered tools: an email drafter with individual voice profiles for each team member, an agreement checker that queries their entire contract library, and a roster and rate engine pulling live from their CRM. Trained all 15 staff in weekly workshops.

Accounting · Noosa

Manual data entry was eating the time they should have spent with clients. Every new engagement meant hours of document processing before anyone could give actual advice.

Deployed a private, on-device AI to process client documents and automate intake. Data never leaves the building.

40% reduction in admin workload.

Construction · Sunshine Coast

Complex quotes were taking hours. The builder couldn't work offline on job sites, and weekends disappeared into quoting.

Built an offline quoting app on tablets with AI-assisted quote generation. Works without reception.

60% faster quotes. Zero errors. Family got their weekends back.

Energy · Vault Energy

Needed market intelligence on renewable energy costs per region. The research required manually pulling data from multiple government and international databases.

Built a market research agent using OpenClaw, plugged into ABS, RapidAPI energy endpoints, and World Bank data. Automated regional cost analysis that previously took days of manual research.
