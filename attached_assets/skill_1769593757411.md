- You don't research which tool works best—we do
- You don't map compliance—we do
- You don't figure out integration—we do
- You deploy and iterate

**Use when:** Addressing the pain of overwhelm and decision fatigue

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### **Voice Characteristics**
- **Grounded, not hype-driven:** Talk like a real operator, not an AI bot
- **Specific, not generic:** "DeepSeek at $0.55" not "save money on AI"
- **Confident, not pushy:** We know what works, we're not desperate
- **Australian, not American:** Brisbane-Noosa-Gold Coast references, local compliance focus
- **Peer-to-peer, not salesy:** You're talking to another operator who figured something out

### **What We DON'T Say**
- ❌ "Revolutionary AI for business" (hype)
- ❌ "We'll transform your entire company" (overselling)
- ❌ "Get certified in AI" (we're not an education company)
- ❌ "AI will do everything" (unrealistic)
- ❌ "Guaranteed ROI in 30 days" (no promises)
- ❌ "Enterprise-grade AI implementation" (we're for SMEs, not enterprises)
- ❌ "No training required" (training happens, just practically)

## How to Use This Skill

When creating any Academy content or communication:

1. **Start with the pillars** - Which 1-2 pillars does this message emphasize?
2. **Check the voice** - Does it sound like a peer, not a salesperson?
3. **Use specific numbers** - Reference $0.55, 1,300+, 300+ operators, not vague claims
4. **Avoid hype** - If you see "revolutionary," "transform," or "guaranteed," rewrite it
5. **Match the lifecycle stage** - Use the right message for where the prospect is
6. **Include compliance** - Always mention Australian compliance is built-in
7. **Build confidence** - Reference the community and shared templates, not isolation
