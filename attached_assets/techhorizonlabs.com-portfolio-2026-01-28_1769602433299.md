#### Manufacturing: Local Manufacturer Reduces Machine Downtime by 30%

A Sunshine Coast-based manufacturer was struggling with unpredictable and costly production halts due to unexpected equipment failures. Our **AI Opportunity Audit** analysed their production line data and identified predictive maintenance as the highest-impact project to improve efficiency and reduce costs. This aligns with our goal to engage with key local sectors, including those involved in the Manufacturing Excellence Forum.

We delivered an **Automation Accelerator** that integrated sensors with a private AI model. The system now monitors equipment performance in real-time and predicts potential failures before they happen, allowing the team to perform maintenance proactively.

#### Healthcare: Allied Health Clinic Guarantees Patient Privacy with AI

A busy allied health clinic on the coast needed to automate their patient appointment reminders and follow-up communication but was rightly concerned about patient privacy. Standard cloud-based AI tools posed an unacceptable risk to their sensitive data. This is a common challenge for regulated industries, a key vertical unlocked by new private AI models.

Our **AI Opportunity Audit** confirmed a private AI solution was the only path forward. The **Automation Accelerator** we built runs entirely on the clinic’s local server. It intelligently manages their appointment schedule, sends personalised reminders, and handles post-visit follow-ups without any patient data ever leaving their control.
